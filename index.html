About Me

Since receiving my B.A. in Philosophy. I've always been interested in technology, more specifically the debate on ethical use of emerging technology. Luckily for me, my work at ISOC-DC provides me with the opportunities to pursue my interests. There, I have encountered issues ranging from the broad topics of internet governance, fragmentation, and net neutrality, to specific issues such as the policing of news feed and fake news.

My goal is to improve my knowledge on emerging internet governance issues and pursue futher education both in the technological and legal aspects of internet governance. In my free time, I like to run, bike, and play soccer. I'm an avid reader, and I enjoy relaxing on the weekends with friends.
